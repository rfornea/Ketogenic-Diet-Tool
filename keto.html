Keto Macro and Calorie Tracker

Welcome to my personal macro calculator! The ketogenic diet is a low-carb, high-fat diet that teaches your body to burn body fat for fuel instead of glucose. On the ketogenic diet, it is a good rule of thumb to stay below 20 net grams of carbohydrates a day and to eat at a caloric deficit of 250-500 calories per day. Visit the r/keto subreddit for more information on the ketogenic diet and use this page or this page to figure out your total daily energy expenditure in an average day.

This tool contains the foods that I most commonly eat. Feel free to view the page source information for this page, copy the HTML file and save it, and make changes to the HTML and Javascript to suit your own personal dietary habits.

Macro and calorie totals will update automatically and be displayed here. Pick some food!
